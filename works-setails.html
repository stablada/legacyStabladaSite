Where am I going?

Python:

I've always had an interest in data collection. My dream as a small child was always to be a chemist and find new types of materials. Luckily, my dreams came true in another way when I was introduced to code at a program in one of my classes and I was hooked. I learned instead of discovering, I could create! Nonetheless, the researcher's desire in me to track and view data lingers on. Thus, I'm working on some data analytics projects through the commonly used Scripting language, Python.

Some ideas I've had include:

  • Web Scraper - Make my life so much easier with searching the web for me without having to do large amounts of research.
  • Stock Tracker (and predictor for good fun!) - I know I'm just a student, but like any other person I want my chance at some winnings in the stock trade! The idea of buying and selling part ownership to fundraise has always fascinated me and I know one thing is for certain: I don't want to lose money. Thus, I want to create a script that analyzes the patterns of a business and marks its predictions for the trends. This will educate me on both stock trade AND Python! Win win!

HTML/CSS/JS

Being a musician and an avid lover of all things classical, I absolutely ADORE the idea of getting to express who you are to others, whether it be through beautiful melodies, incredible stories, or even cooking. Because of this, I've aspired to make my own websites for a while now, and I intend on practicing my UI/UX skills in a meaningful way so I can not only help myself get out there, but later on help others like you get your words or business out there!

  • Freelance Web Design - If I work for others, I'll be learning and getting paid in the meantime!
  • Fully Made Responsive Website - I want my site to look fancy!

Nonetheless, I truly look forward to getting to share my experience with you! Thank you for reading my page!
